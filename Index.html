About our project:

We are GH Games, and we’re making great game about radio controlled cars. If you ask man, doesn’t matter how old he is, but anyway, with high possibility he could say that he wanna buy rc car. Why? It’s awesome feelings while you’re driving your scaled rc model, and you can feel your car.

Our game will be about that feelings and emotions, because anyone can try that car, what they wanna buy, or already have, and just ride on in inside our game! That’s great, doesn’t it?

We’re trying to make realistic physics of cars, realistic controls, movement on the wheels and in the air, we will make and gas powered cars too! Our cars will have different classes and variations of chassis to choose that one, that should suit for current race.
