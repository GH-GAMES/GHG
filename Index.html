About our project:

We are GH Games, and we’re making great game about radio controlled cars. If you ask man, doesn’t matter how old he is, but anyway, with high possibility he could say that he wanna buy rc car. Why? It’s awesome feelings while you’re driving your scaled rc model, and you can feel your car.

Our game will be about that feelings and emotions, because anyone can try that car, what they wanna buy, or already have, and just ride on in inside our game! That’s great, doesn’t it?

We’re trying to make realistic physics of cars, realistic controls, movement on the wheels and in the air, we will make and nitro powered cars too! Our cars will have different classes and variations of chassis to choose that one, that should suit for current race.

We at GH Games are ready to present you a game about radio-controlled cars. RC:CM are machine maniacs.

If you ask a person what he, regardless of age, will answer that he wants to buy himself a radio-controlled car.

Why? It's an incredible feeling when you control your own scale RC model, competing with friends and other users. In our game, everyone can choose the car they dream of! That is why we approached the creation of the game very carefully: realistic car physics, thoughtful solutions, unique design, different classes and chassis options. All this to give you the race you dream of!
