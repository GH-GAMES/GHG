about us:

GH Games:

As a leading game development company, GH Games has a clear goal of creating exceptional gaming experiences that appeal to gamers around the world. The identity and values of our brand are based on our unwavering commitment to creating first-class games. Our motto is simple but powerful: "We will do what you like."

History of the company:

GH Games was founded by a group of passionate and experienced game developers who shared a common dream of creating exciting and unforgettable gaming experiences. Since our founding, we have been committed to pushing the boundaries of creativity and innovation in the gaming industry.

Our team:

GH Games has a talented team, each member of which plays a key role in bringing our idea to life. Meet Max, our dedicated team leader, an all-round expert in 3D modeling and Unreal Engine 5 development. Next to him is Anton, a brilliant UE5 programmer who makes sure the technical aspects of our games are top notch. Pavel, our talented designer and artist-musician, adds his creativity to every aspect of our projects. Finally, we have AcidMerph, our exceptional render artist, who enhances the visual appeal of our games.

Our Vision/Mission:

The ultimate goal is to release a brilliant game before 2024 that will win the hearts of our players. We see GH Games as a driving force in the gaming industry, inspiring positive change and setting new standards of excellence.
